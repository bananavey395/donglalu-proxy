Frequently Asked Questions


Why aren't some sites working?
Not every site is guaranteed to work, as various sites might have unique properties that make it hard to proxy. Sites that don't work include those with react.js (a JS library used for UI's) & sites that depend on window.location and are minified.

Is Discord supported?
Yes, Discord is currently supported with the latest build of Womginx.

Is this Open Source?
Yes, Donglalu Proxy is currently open source. You can check it out at my github https://github.com/bananavey395

Why is this called Donglalu Proxy?
It's a long story, but basically it's named after a nickname I gave to someone.
